What's a fansub group?

A fansub (short for fan-subtitled) is a version of a foreign film or foreign television program, typically anime or dorama which has been translated by fans (as opposed to an officially licensed translation done by paid professionals) and subtitled into a language usually other than that of the original.

These fansub groups mainly make contents of Minecraft YouTubers such as BadBoyHalo and Skeppy, and the part I play is the translator, whose job is to translate and ensure the translational quality of the script and signs respectively.

I was so devoted to this because I was obsessed with Minecraft YouTubers and eagerly wanted to do something for them, Translating and reposting their videos was the best way I could think of. A lot of people don't have access to websites like YouTube in my country, and doing this helps both YouTubers and the audiences.

I made more than 50 videos with them in the past 2 years. I've made a lot of friends online during this process and improved my English a little bit by doing this. I even started my own account. Until this day, I still work for three of those fansub groups.
